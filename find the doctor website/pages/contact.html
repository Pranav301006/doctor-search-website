Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by searching for doctors, selecting your preferred doctor, and clicking on the "Book Appointment" button. Fill in your details and preferred time slot.

Are all doctors verified?

Yes, all doctors on our platform are verified healthcare professionals with valid medical licenses and qualifications.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 2 hours before the scheduled time by contacting our support team.

Is there a booking fee?

No, booking appointments through DocFind is completely free. You only pay the consultation fee directly to the doctor.
